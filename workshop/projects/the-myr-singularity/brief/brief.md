# The Myr Singularity Brief

## Project Identity

The Myr Singularity is an artifact combo-control engine disguised as a Myr tribal deck.

Commander: Urtet, Remnant of Memnarch.

The deck uses Myr and artifact bodies as resources, not merely as attackers.

## Sprint 1 Goal

Preserve the Myr identity while improving:

- Explosiveness
- Consistency
- Mana fixing
- Resilience

## Scope

This brief initializes the Sprint 1 project direction only.

It does not add decklist content, card facts, analysis, recommendations, decisions, or code.
